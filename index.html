The Opportunity

Own at the Gateway to the Venice of America

A South Florida icon for over 50 years, Pier Sixty‑Six is being completely reimagined as a luxury waterfront destination. The inaugural offering presents 62 homes with privileged access to the resort, marina promenade, restaurants, shops, and superyacht dockage.
